The OMNI LH 96 is a fully automated liquid handling and homogenizer workstation.

The OMNI LH 96 has a modular design, small footprint, and brush-less motors for automated homogenizing, but also integrates multiple liquid handling steps, such as weighing, diluting, and pipetting into one end-to-end system.

Fully customizable, the OMNI LH 96 can increase productivity, reduce errors, and improve results, completing large projects faster than otherwise possible.

  • ELIMINATE CROSS CONTAMINATION

    The LH 96 can utilize 7 mm Omni Tips™ plastic disposable generator probes. A fresh tip is selected for each sample and then discarded once processing is complete, thus eliminating cross contamination.

    Omni Tips remove the tedious task of disassembly and cleaning of stainless-steel generator probes. With the ability to have a one-use generator probe, Omni Tips are an ideal solution for applications targeted at nucleic acid extraction, where reproducibility is critical or those where false positives need to be avoided at all costs.

    Confidence in knowing that precious samples & expensive reagents were not wasted

  • HUMAN LIKE GENERATOR PROBE MOVEMENT

    The system was designed to provide automated homogenization using the same generator probe movement as a manual handheld homogenizer operated by a laboratory technician.

    Whisper Drive Technology

    Featuring Whisper Drive Technology, the unit is equipped with 4 x 100-watt brushless motors and is virtually maintenance-free and ultra-quiet during operation. With whisper drive technology the motor holds processing speed to +/- 1% to ensure reproducibility and accuracy across all samples regardless of fluctuations in sample size and viscosity.

Summary of features

The small footprint fits into most bio-safety cabinets and fume hoods. User-friendly programming makes saving, recalling, or changing existing methods easy. Omni patented Whisper Drive Technology™ with brush-less motors delivers maintenance-free, ultra-quiet, maximum processing power.

  • Automated homogenization of up to ninety-six samples
  • Liquid handling of up to four separate liquids
  • Pipetting and liquid transfer
  • Process 250 μl to 40 mL in 1.5 to 50 mL tubes
  • Create custom sample processing profiles in the LH 96 software
  • Unique oscillating motion to ensure complete homogenization
  • Variable speed from 500 to 28,000 rpm
  • Compatible with 7 mm OMNI Tips™ Plastic Omni Tips™
  • Compatible with 5, 7 or 10 mm stainless steel generator probes
  • Ultrasonic cleaning bath for stainless steel probes
